About ZackFinance

ZackFinance Marketplace is a digital platform created to connect entrepreneurs seeking investment with investors who are ready to support high‑potential business ideas.

Our mission is to empower African entrepreneurs by giving them access to funding, mentorship, and market visibility.

We believe in transparency, opportunity, and innovation. Through this platform, we aim to bridge the gap between ideas and capital.
